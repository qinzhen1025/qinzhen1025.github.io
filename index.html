About me

I received the B.M. degree and B.S. degree from Peking University, in 2025.
I am currently pursuing a M.P.H. at Peking University, Beijing, China.

My research focuses on applying mathematical and statistical models to understand the transmission dynamics of infectious diseases. Furthermore, I am also interested in disease forecasting, epidemiological analysis, and public health intervention strategies.
